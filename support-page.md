# Regulate Support

Hi — thanks for using Regulate. This page is where to come if something's not working, you have a question, or you need to reach a human.

## What Regulate does, in one paragraph

Regulate is a wellness app that helps you spot patterns in your daily habits that may be raising your stress response. You answer a short quiz about your symptoms and habits, the app produces your **Cortisol Score** and an **archetype** that describes your current pattern, and you get a 30-day plan of small daily habit changes. Each day you check in, log what you did, and your score updates. It's a self-improvement tool, not a medical device.

## Frequently asked questions

### What's the Cortisol Score and is it accurate?
The Cortisol Score is a 0–100 number computed from your self-reported answers about symptoms and habits. It's a signal of how much your daily routines may be pushing your stress system, **not** a clinical measurement of cortisol. We don't take blood, saliva, or any physiological readings.

### Is Regulate medical advice?
No. Regulate is a wellness and lifestyle app. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you have a health concern, talk to a qualified healthcare professional.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the App Store app → tap your Apple ID at the top → tap **Subscriptions** → tap **Regulate** → tap **Cancel Subscription**. Cancellation stops future charges; you keep access until the end of the current period. Apple handles refunds — we can't process them for you.

### How do I restore my subscription on a new device?
Sign in with the same Apple ID, open Regulate, go to the paywall screen and tap **Restore Purchases**.

### How do I export my data?
Open the app → tap the gear icon to open Settings → **Data** → **Export my data**. We'll generate a JSON file with every row tied to your account; you can save it to Files or share it.

### How do I delete my account?
Open the app → Settings → **Data** → **Delete account**. You'll be asked to type a confirmation phrase. Deletion is permanent and immediate: every row tied to your account is removed, your subscription is cancelled (where applicable), and your Apple Sign In link is revoked.

### I forgot to cancel before being charged.
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit https://reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find the Regulate charge, and request a refund. Apple reviews refund requests case by case.
